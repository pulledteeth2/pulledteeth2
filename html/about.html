about the great arms

As years have progressed, the companies Navatrols and Hensodyne have grown from their original purposes of pharmaceutical research and aerospace (respectively), to the top two weapons and defense companies in the world. Collectively, these two companies are referred to as the "Great Arms".

These pages document the technological advancements and societal shifts created and/or caused by the Great Arms.
