Aliysies

Aliysies are humans born with a Cytozine pattern in their DNA that gives them certain abilities. The make-up of their Cytozine pattern determines the kind of abilities they may have. Some aliysies go their whole lives without ever knowing they were considered aliysies. In most governments, aliysies are not seen as human. They are often kept as slaves by the wealthy, using them either for menial labor, mercenary work, guarding, and sexual pleasure. Because of their negative treatment, most aliysies avoid advertising their status in order to continue living within society.
