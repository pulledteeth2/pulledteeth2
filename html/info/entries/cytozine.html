Cytozine

Cytozine is a chemical compound discovered in 1963 by Hensodyne researcher Conrad Roswell. Cytozine exists in each aliysies, each with a unique pattern determining the special abilities that aliysies might possess. General Grade Cytozine, which acts as a poisonous deppresant for aliysies. In 2019, Hensodyne became the first of the Great Arms to start offering Cytozine-tipped ammunition to general consumers.
