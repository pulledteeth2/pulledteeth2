K93-M52

K93-M52 is the first and only successful Aliysies Conversion. It was completed by Navatrols in 2007, and active in various missions until it was lost in 2014. A $17.9M USD/$27.8M AUD bounty was offered for it's return. In October 2021, the identity of K93-M52 was learned and publicized, leading to a three-week manhunt throughout the state of New York. K93-M52 returned itself in November 2021. K93-M52 was once again lost in June 2022.
